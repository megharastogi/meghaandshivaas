Megha & Shivaas are getting married
Our Story
Megha and Shivaas met during their first semester at Inderprastha Engg. College, where they had enrolled in what they thought was a 4yr B.Tech degree, but ended up with a bachelors degree and life partner in tow. From those evening momo snacks, to the days spend bunking in the wings of the amphitheater, to late night (err.. early morning for Shivaas!) skype calls, Megha and Shivaas have come a long way since that chilly November night, 2 weeks before final exams, when Shivaas spent 6 not so futile hours convicing Megha for that first date... A chapter that started years back now comes to an end.. And having spent the better part of the last 6 years together, Megha and Shivaas now embark on a new chapter in their lives....
